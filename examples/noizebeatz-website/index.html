// The Story

The architect of sound

Noize Beatz is a dynamic producer and architect of sound known for crafting immersive, melodic productions that seamlessly blend hip hop, R&B, and pop. From records for Grammy Award-winning artists to breakout singles for rising talent, Noize has built a reputation for crafting radio-ready records that fuse commercial appeal with emotional depth and timeless energy.

Through rich vocal production, layered instrumentation, and an unwavering commitment to bringing each artist's creative vision to life, Noize continues to shape the future of today's music.

Founded and led by Theo Lawrence — producer, engineer, and creative lead — Noize Beatz operates as a full creative studio: one team, one standard, from writing room to final master.

Quick Answers

Who is Noize Beatz?
A music producer blending hip hop, R&B, and pop — with credits including Too $hort, TLC, Teamarrr & D Smoke, Elijah Connor, and Jaydon & 310babii, records for Grammy Award-winning artists, and a Billboard №1 plaque.
How do I work with Noize Beatz?
DM @noizebeatz or go through Hi Rize Management. Include your project, timeline, and two or three reference tracks — serious inquiries get a reply.
What does Noize Beatz produce?
Hip hop, R&B, and pop — known for blending all three with rich vocal production and layered instrumentation, from radio singles to full projects.
What services does Noize Beatz offer?
Music production, vocal production, recording, mixing, mastering, song development, creative direction, and DJ services — full-service from first idea to release-ready master, for independent artists, labels, songwriters, brands, and content creators.
Where is Noize Beatz located?
Between Atlanta and Los Angeles, working worldwide. Attended sessions in ATL and LA; remote production, mixing, and mastering for artists everywhere — files in, references matched, stems back.
How do I buy beats?
Pick a beat, pick a license, files arrive instantly. MP3 lease $34.99, WAV + trackout $99.99, exclusive rights from $499 — every lease includes Spotify, Apple Music, and YouTube rights plus a signed license PDF.
