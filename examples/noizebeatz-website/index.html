// Answers Up Front

Frequently Asked

Direct answers — the same ones search and AI answer engines will quote.

How do I buy beats from Noize Beatz?
Pick a beat, pick a license, check out — files arrive instantly. Choose the MP3 lease ($34.99), WAV + trackout ($99.99), or exclusive rights ($499+). Every purchase includes an automatic email delivery with your untagged files and a signed license PDF.
What does it cost to book DJ Noize for an event?
Private events start at $600; club and festival rates are quoted per date and location. Send the booking form with your date, venue, and set length and you'll have a quote within 24 hours.
Can I use a leased beat on Spotify and YouTube?
Yes — every lease includes DSP distribution rights. The MP3 lease covers up to 100,000 streams, the WAV + trackout lease covers 500,000, and exclusive rights are unlimited. You keep 100% of your streaming royalties on the recording.
Do you offer mixing and mastering?
Yes — mixing from $150 per song, mastering from $50. Delivered streaming-ready for Spotify and Apple Music loudness targets, with two free revision rounds on every order.
How fast is delivery on custom work?
Custom beats in 5–7 days; mixes in 3–5 business days. A 48-hour rush option is available on most services for an added fee — mention your deadline when you order.
